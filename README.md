# Insurance-Risk-Claims-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI
An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes insurance policies, claims, customer demographics, and vehicle data to uncover risk patterns, monitor claim trends, and support data-driven decision-making through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.
Key Insights:
Private-use vehicles account for the highest total claim amount.
BMW, Audi, and Honda generate the largest insurance claims.
Claim amounts are distributed almost equally across different coverage zones.
Customers aged 56–65 contribute the highest claim amount.
Vehicle model years around 2013–2017 show higher claim values.
Single customers generate the highest total claims across most education levels.
